The Current State of Pokemon TCG

What is going on right now in the TCG World?

I have been a longtime collector since I was a child for Pokemon cards, plushies, and anything related to it. Right now, everyone seems to agree that collecting as a hobby in this is cool and very fun to do. I agree, I've been doing it myself for a while now too. However, sometimes I can't believe that there are some people who either take advantage of the hobby or cause harm because of it. For example, There was Renton Pokemon Violence over a disupte of Pokemon cards. I know this hobby is fun and made for collectors, kids, and all of the above. However, when I see violence such as this, I begin to ask when is there going to be more regulations in place for things like this to stop?
